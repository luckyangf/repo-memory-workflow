# LOG

Append one entry per meaningful manual step or automated relay round. Never
overwrite existing entries.

## Round Entry Template

### Round N - YYYY-MM-DD HH:mm

- Completed:
  - What changed or was verified?
- Failed:
  - What failed, if anything?
- Risks:
  - Current uncertainty or regression risk.
- Next:
  - Recommended next action for `.ai/NEXT.md`.
